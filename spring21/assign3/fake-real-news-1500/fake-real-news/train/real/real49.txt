Trump on Twitter: Hillary is 'Brainwashed'
Donald Trump took to Twitter Saturday morning where he launched his latest attacks on Hillary Clinton, calling her "brainwashed" after she acknowledged that she "short-circuited" when making a misstatement.

When speaking to members of the National Association of Black Journalists and the National Association of Hispanic Journalists in Washington Friday, Clinton sought to "clarify and explain" a statement she made that FBI Director James Comey said that her previous answers about her private email use were "truthful."

"I may have short-circuited [her answers] and for that I will try to clarify," Clinton said.
